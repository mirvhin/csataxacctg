Our Services

Tax Preparation and Filing

Preparation and filing of monthly, quarterly, and annual BIR tax returns (e.g., VAT, Percentage Tax, Income Tax, Withholding Tax). We ensure your tax obligations are submitted accurately and on time.

BIR Registration and Compliance

Assistance with registering your business with the Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR), including securing COR (Certificate of Registration), books of accounts, and official receipts. We also handle updates and closures.

Bookkeeping Services

Weekly, monthly or annual recording of business transactions using manual or computerized systems. We maintain clean, organized records that comply with BIR standards.

Tax Advisory and Consultation

Audit Assistance

Support during internal or BIR audits, including document preparation, reconciliation, and representation, ensuring your compliance and reducing the risk of findings.

Freelancer and Online Seller Tax Services

Specialized packages for freelancers, vloggers, and online sellers (e.g., Shopee, Lazada) who need to register and file taxes correctly under the TRAIN Law.
